About Our Club

Founded in 2015, Aperture Club brings together photography enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you're a professional photographer or just starting out, our club offers a supportive environment to learn, grow, and share your passion for photography.

We organize regular photo walks, workshops, and critique sessions to help members improve their skills and explore different photography styles and techniques.
